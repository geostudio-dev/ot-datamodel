Attribute Scope Type
consolidationDegree Public CGI_Term
nillable=True
sequenceNumber=1
Notes: A property that specifies the degree to which an aggregation of EarthMaterial particles is a distinct solid material. Consolidation and induration are related concepts specified by this property. They define a continuum from unconsolidated material to very hard rock. Induration is the degree to which a consolidated material is made hard, operationally determined by how difficult it is to break a piece of the material. Consolidated materials may have varying degrees of induration (NADMSC, 2004)<br/><br/>
