Links are the basic curve elements of a network topology. They are always associated to a topological complex, via the topology:TP_DirectedEdge attribute and through it, potentially to a geometric complex (topology.geometry:GM_Curve).<br/><br/><br/><br/>-- At least one direction of the NT_Link is<br/>-- traversable in the network<br/><br/>{Not(NT_Link.isTraversable) implies NT_Link.negate().isTraversable}<br/><br/>{Not(NT_Link.negate().isTraversable) implies NT_Link.isTraversable}<br/><br/>If it has an underlying geometry<br/>it is a measured curve.<br/><br/>{topology.topo.geometry.isTypeOf(MC_Curve)}<br/>
