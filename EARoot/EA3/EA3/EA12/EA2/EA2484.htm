Class of places in the real world.<br/>Examples : street, junction between two streets, building, country<br/>A spatial referencing system using geographic identifiers shall comprise one or more location types (which may be related, for example by nesting), with a common theme. Each location type shall be uniquely identified by means of one or more geographic identifier types.<br/>
