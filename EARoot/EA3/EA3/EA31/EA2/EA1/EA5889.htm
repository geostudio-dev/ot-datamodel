The high level structure of the LCML consists of the relationships between the LC_LandCoverClassificationSystemMetaDescription metaobject and the aggregation of a set of LC_LandCover metaobjects.  The LC_LandCoverClassificationSystemMetaDescription metaobject may be instantiated to produce a LCCS as represented by the class LC_LandCoverClassificationSystem. The individual LC_LandCover metaobjects may be instantiated to produce LC_LandCoverClass(s) which correspond to individual classes in a LCCS. LC_LandCoverClass is a subtype of CL_LegendClass as defined in ISO 19144-1:2009. <br/><br/>
