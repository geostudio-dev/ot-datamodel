The LC_Element metaobject has two subtypes representing the two basic types of land cover. <br/><br/>The metalanguage operates by representing land cover classes in an LCCS in terms of the subtypes of LC_Element or set of LC_Element(s), with associated attributes and characteristics. That is, a particular land cover class in an LCCS may be modelled by the metaobjects in the LCML and compared with another land cover class in a different LCCS by examining the relationship between the models of the two classes in the metamodel. For example, a particular concept may have a detailed explicit class in one LCCS but it may only be able to represent it by a more general class in another LCCS. In this case it would be easy to determine from the representations of the two LCCS in the LCML that one class was a specialization of the other.<br/><br/>
