The metaobject structure establishes the rules for the aggregation of occurrences of LC_Element, into the metaobject LC_LandCover, that when instantiated produces a class in a Land Cover Classification System. Occurences of LC_Element may be combined in layers the <i>stratumComposition</i> relationship between LC_Element and LC_Stratum. The relationship of the LC_Elements within a layer are given by the <i>inSameStrataRelationship</i>. Several types of relationships including a <i>sequentialTemporalRelationship</i>, a <i>conditional</i> relationship and an <i>exclusive</i> (XOR) relationship may be described by the LC_ElementRelationInSameStratum association class. There may be any number of strata. The relationship between the strata are given by the <i>interStrataRelationship</i>. Several types of relationships including a <i>conditional</i> relationship and an <i>onTop</i> relationship may be described. Elements in a stratum may be built upon the same baseline or they may be indicated to be on top of another stratum. <br/>
