The LC_RocksSurfaceElement metalanguage object is a subtype of LC_NaturalSurfaceElement. This metaclass, when instantiated, allows the generation of a general land cover feature class in an LCCS for all consolidated natural surfaces. It has two subtypes LC_BareRocksAndCoarseFragments and LC_Hardpans. <br/>
