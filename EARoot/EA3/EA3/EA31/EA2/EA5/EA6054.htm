The LC_NameArbitrationCriteria metaobject is a component of LC_FloristicAspects through the relationship <i>nameAttributionCriteria</i> and has two subtypes LC_SinglePlantSpecies and LC_GroupOfPlantSpecies. These two subtypes correspond to the two cases where a floristic name is derived from a single plant species or a group of plant species. This component may be used to refine the metaobject corresponding to an LC_floristicAspects class with respect to species name to permit the generation of a more specific land cover feature class in an LCCS.<br/><br/>
