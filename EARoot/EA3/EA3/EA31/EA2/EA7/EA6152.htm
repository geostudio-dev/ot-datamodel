The class LC_LCMLRegister specifies information about the register itself. It is a subclass of the class RE_Register which is defined in ISO 19135. It inherits 6 attributes from ISO 19135. Two of the attributes are specialized below. The other attributes are taken directly from ISO 19135.<br/>
