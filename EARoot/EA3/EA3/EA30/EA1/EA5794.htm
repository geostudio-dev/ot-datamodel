MF_TemporalGeometry is a specialization of MF_OneParamGeometry in which the parameter is time as expressed by TM_Coordinate.  TM_Coordinate is specified in ISO 19108; it expresses time as a multiple of a single unit of measure such as year, day, or second.  <br/>
